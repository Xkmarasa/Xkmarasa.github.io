Our Story

Mediterranean Blue is a company that focuses on luxury real estate in Spain, offering properties that exude the Mediterranean lifestyle, with a specific focus on the Costa Blanca. All of the properties that we represent, whether it be a villa or light filled beachside apartment, present spectacular views of the Mediterranean and have access to beaches, restaurants, health facilities, schools and amenities to enjoy a complete Spanish lifestyle.

The Costa Blanca is made up of a mix of laid back and lively beach towns like Calpe, Altea, Benissa, Moraira, Teulada and Denia. Bringing the highest quality and design to our offerings, and unmatched location, we have had the honor of collaborating with a leader in architecture and innovation, a renowned developer in Spain, and known throughout Europe.

We dedicate our time to modern properties offering privacy, tranquility and exceptional quality. We help our clients to find the ideal location and best property with all of the comforts to fit their needs.
